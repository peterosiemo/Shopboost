Business Terms

Our glossary of business terminology will explain some of the meanings of the most commonly used business phrases and
business definitions – so talking to your accountant should be a bit easier in the future.

ACCOUNTING PERIOD
The time over which financial statements and accounts are prepared, usually measured over months, quarters or years.
ACCOUNTS PAYABLE
Sums of money owed by your company.
ACCOUNTS RECEIVABLE
Money owed to your company.
EQUITY
The difference between the company’s assets and liabilities.
Variable Costs
Variable costs are expenses that fluctuate based on your volume of business. They include: Shipping Commissions Supplies Hourly wages
